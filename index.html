Kaminakia
⭐ Just across the road
Just across the road lies what feels like our own private beach ... the very place that gave Kaminakia its name. Crystalline waters glimmer in the morning light and whisper softly at dusk. It's the perfect place for early risers and twilight dreamers alike, and a favorite haunt for curious snorkelers exploring its secret depths.

Emplisi
🚶 30 min · 🚗 5 min
A 30-minute walk or a short drive leads to a landscape of slanting white rocks, perfect for soaking up the sun's warmth. A caravan beach bar stands ready for those seeking a laid-back refreshment amidst the dazzle of the blue.

Kimilia
🚗 Via Emplisi + 10-min walk
A cozy little hideaway, tucked away from the beaten path. To find it, slip through the gateway at the back of Emplisi beach and follow the wooded trail that curves around the peninsula. You may also approach from the road; simply park and enjoy a casual 10-minute stroll to discover this hidden gem.
